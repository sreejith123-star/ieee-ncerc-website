Who We Are

IEEE SB NCERC

IEEE SB NCERC was revamped on 7th January 2021 and was inaugurated by Susan Kathy Land, IEEE President 2021.

We have hosted impactful workshops, seminars, and technical competitions. We provide members opportunities to meet and collaborate with global professionals.

IEEE SB NCERC is pledged to inspire young minds through advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

What We Stand For

Our Mission

To inspire and enable the next generation of engineers through access to world-class resources, networking, and hands-on experiences.

  • Exercise & adapt knowledge for the common good
  • Develop managerial and organizational skills
  • Celebrate the spirit of working together
👁️
Our Vision

To advance technological innovation for the benefit of humanity — building leaders who leverage technology to create meaningful impact.

  • Foster global community of engineers
  • Bridge academia and industry
  • Empower through technology and innovation
